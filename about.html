About Us
Jace Wright :

Jace Wright was born in Idaho Falls to Brian and Shellie Wright and raised 15 minutes away in Shelley, Idaho. He is the oldest of 4 children. He has been lucky enough to call the Eastern side of Idaho his home for 26 years now. He has a love for sports, mostly basketball, football and golf, but loves water activities in the summer like boating and jet skiing and snowboarding in the winter. He has a background in the Food/Retail business and has a lot of experience with people and working with them. He started working for his parents at the age of 13 and quickly gained the skills that would later benefit him. At the age of 21 he became one of the youngest General Managers for a company called Kneaders Bakery and Cafe'. He loves people and serving them in any capacity weather it’s in his religious callings or business dealings. "The success in any business is contingent on the relationships you build" - Jace
Jace has a beautiful little family. His wife Chelsie and 3 little ones Chayzlee, Kaysten, and Zuri, with more to come in the future. "They are my world" -Jace
Jace is an entrepreneur at heart and loves the idea of having control of time. Real Estate plays a huge part in that and he loves the flexibility it gives him to work with clients in any way they need and also have a solid family life. He is anxious to make a name for himself in the real estate world.
